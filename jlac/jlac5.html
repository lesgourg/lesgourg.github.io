Reaching LAPP and LAPTH by car

    First Step: until the highway exit Annecy Nord

    - starting from Geneva: after the border follow Annecy in green (not the highway). Cross the villages called Cruseilles and Allonzier. Just after Allonzier follow on your right Annecy in blue (highway). Exit from the highway at Annecy Nord and pay 0.70 EUR.

    - starting from Lausanne: take the Geneva highway until the customs of Bardonnex. After the border follow Annecy in green (not the highway). Cross the villages called Cruseilles and Allonzier. Just after Allonzier follow on your right Annecy in blue (highway). Exit from the highway at Annecy Nord and pay 0.70 EUR.

    - starting from Grenoble: take the highway towards Chambery and then Annecy. Exit from the highway at Annecy Nord.
    Second step: from the highway exit Annecy Nord to LAPP and LAPTH

      - first roundabout: 2nd exit direction THONES

      - second roundabout: 3nd exit direction THONES

      - after 1.5 km: take on your right, direction THONES

      - roundabout: 2nd exit direction THONES

      - after 3.5 km, roundabout: 2nd exit direction Espace Rencontre (under a wooden bridge)

      - after 500m, roundabout: 3nd exit direction Domaine Universitaire, LAPP

      - after 500m, roundabout: 1nd exit: you are at LAPP/LAPTH

    The room Salle des Sommets is at the last floor of the round building on your left.
