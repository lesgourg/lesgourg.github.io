Reaching LAPP and LAPTH by car

When arriving nearby Annecy from the road of Geneva, or from the exit Annecy Nord on the highway, the trick is to follow always the direction THONES rather than Annecy-Le-Vieux. After many intersections and roundabouts, you will be directed to a new express road, which seems to leave completely the town in the direction of the mountains. Go on anyway! The express road ends up on a roundabout. Take the second way out, called Sur les Bois, and then the third small road on your left, called Chemin de Bellevue, with an indication for LAPP and Domaine Universitaire. After 200 meters, you will find on your right the entrance and car park of LAPP and LAPTH.

If you get lost, you may follow the indications for the Domaine Universitaire (IUT) or for the Hôtel Arc-en-ciel in Annecy-Le-Vieux. It will bring you 100 meters downhill from LAPP.
